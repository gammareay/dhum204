Communism Project

Zach Chhabria | DHUM 204

For the final project I am looking to extend on my textual project for the most part, while using arts from the two other projects. My first project focused on Fukuyama and his work where he was talking about the threat of communism and how we need to focus on a liberal democracy so that we can fight communism. For my final project I want to keep that same idea but now start to look at how other philosophers and political theorists were also worrying about and predicting how we could oppose the red scare and communism. I would like to see common trends of what they were saying but also look at it from a chronological perspective and see where the peak of the fear in their writing was and any correlations that that may have, The first tool I am going to use is voyant to break down all of the articles and papers. From there I am going to use ArcGIS to map out where these places are and see if there are more of them closer to Russia and the source of communism or if there is no direct correlation. Finally I am going to put it all onto a timeline to also look for correlations and see if there are more happening later in time towards the peak of the red scare
