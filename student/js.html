An Analysis of the Drew Acorn's "Beyond the Bubble"

Jakob Spotts | DHUM 204

My project will be analyzing the “Beyond the Bubble” section in the Drew Acorn. I will be looking at the stories that section covers, identify what news companies are cited (AP, NYTimes, etc.), and mapping those locations to see how “beyond” the Drew Acorn journalists.
