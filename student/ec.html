Reproduction of Inequality in American Sport

Eric Castner | DHUM 204

My final project will research governance, space, and language, specifically that of which has been institutionalized, built and reproduced inequality within American Golf. Specifically I will shape this project around the research question, how do public and private golf institutions establish access through space, language, and governance, and how do these designs reproduce inequalities related to class and gender? This project will look at golf beyond a sport. It will rather examine it through the lens of a cultural institution that exists with systems of economic oppression, gender exclusion, and protected property.
