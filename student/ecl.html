New Jersey Conservation

Ella Clancy | DHUM 204

I plan to continue to explore locations of nature preserves in New Jersey to answer the question of how much space is being dedicated to nature conservation today, as well as possibly creating a timeline of political attitudes toward conservation in New Jersey to further visualize how public opinion of environmentalism can boost or diminish conservation efforts.
